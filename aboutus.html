ABOUT US

Our Story

Welcome to Vision, your portal to the realm of Egyptian craftsmanship and innovation. Based in Egypt, Vision is deeply rooted in the country's rich tradition of artistry, with a focus on glass and porcelain decoration.

Located in Samannoud City, historically renowned for pottery manufacturing, our firm embraces the evolution of craftsmanship. Since the 1960s, Samannoud has flourished as a hub for glass making and ornamentation, competing with international giants.

With a commitment to modernization, Vision revitalizes Egypt's glassmaking sector, equipping local manufacturers with cutting-edge technologies and materials. Since our establishment in 2002, we've spearheaded research and development efforts to enhance competitiveness on a global scale.

Participation in industry events and exhibitions has cemented our role in facilitating collaboration and innovation. Egyptian manufacturers, empowered by our support, now excel on the global stage, from North America to Asia.

This website marks a pivotal moment for Vision, signaling our ambition to expand globally. By showcasing Egyptian raw materials and finished products, we aim to captivate international markets and uphold Egypt's legacy of craftsmanship.
